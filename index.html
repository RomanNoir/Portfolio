BYE BYE IMPERFECTIONS!
Retouch | 2025
Roman Noir

I specialize in high-quality skin retouching, focusing on acne removal, blemish correction, and overall skin refinement while preserving natural texture. My approach is tailored to the specific needs of each photo, whether for personal portraits, beauty campaigns, or professional branding.

For acne and blemish removal, I use Adobe Photoshop’s Healing Brush, Clone Stamp, and Frequency Separation techniques, carefully eliminating imperfections while maintaining realistic skin details.

For skin tone correction and evening out discoloration, I apply Dodge & Burn and selective color adjustments in Camera Raw and Photoshop, ensuring a smooth yet natural complexion.

For final refinements, I manually adjust sharpness, contrast, and lighting to enhance the overall image quality while keeping it true to life. Each retouch is adapted to the intended use of the image, whether for social media, beauty portfolios, or commercial purposes, ensuring a polished and professional result without an over-processed look.
